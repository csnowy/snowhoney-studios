Refund Policy

Last updated: October 1, 2025

1. General Policy

We want you to be happy with your purchase. If we fail to deliver your service as promised, you may be eligible for a full or partial refund under the terms below.

2. Packages

  • Mockup Only: Fully refundable before delivery. Once the mockup is delivered, the order is final.
  • One-Page / Two-Page / Three-Page Builds: Fully refundable if work has not yet begun. Once we start design or development, refunds may be prorated at our discretion.
  • Turnaround Guarantee: If we fail to deliver your finished site within 7 days of order (unless delayed by missing client information), you are entitled to a full refund.

3. Hosting

  • Hosting Only Orders: May be cancelled within 7 days of purchase for a full refund, provided setup has not begun. After setup begins, hosting is non-refundable but you may cancel future billing anytime via the client portal.
  • Hosting with Site Builds: Hosting charges begin 7 days after checkout or once your free trial ends (depending on your package). Hosting is billed monthly and is non-refundable once a billing cycle has started, but you can cancel future renewals anytime.

4. Add-Ons

  • Extra Pages: Refundable only if no work has begun. Once design or coding has started, extra pages are non-refundable.

5. Coupons & Discounts

If you applied a coupon, promotional discount, or mockup credit, refunds will not exceed the amount you actually paid. The value of discounts themselves is non-refundable.

6. Client Portal

You can manage hosting cancellations, upgrades, and edits at any time through the Snowhoney Studios client portal. Portal access is provided to the email used at checkout.

7. Contact

Questions about refunds? Contact us at support@snowhoneystudios.ca.
