Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: [Sept 17th, 2025]

1. Website Builds

Refund available only if canceled before work begins. Once started, payments are non-refundable. If we fail our 7-day launch guarantee (and you provided content on time), you may request a full refund.

Requests that fall outside the scope of the original package (such as additional pages or complex integrations) are not included in the original fee and will be billed separately. Payments for add-on work are non-refundable once started.

2. Mockups

The $99 mockup is non-refundable once delivered.

3. Hosting Plans

Hosting subscriptions renew monthly via Stripe. Cancel anytime, but no refunds for the current billing cycle. Service remains active until period end.

4. Domains & Emails

Domains and business emails purchased on your behalf are non-refundable. You may request transfer at any time.

5. Chargebacks

If a dispute is filed, we will provide Stripe with evidence of work performed and communications.

5a. Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds will be issued through Stripe and may take 5–10 business days to appear on your original payment method, depending on your bank or card provider.

6. Contact

Email us at billing@snowhoneystudios.ca for cancellations or refund questions.
