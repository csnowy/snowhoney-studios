FAQ

How fast can I launch?

7 days from your order AT MOST! If not, we guarantee a FULL refund AND your webpage - no cost! However, if you need it sooner we have a rush fee available!

What do you need from me?

Business name, services, locations, 2–3 photos, and how you want people to contact you (call, form, booking link). You'll be prompted with a form containing all of this and more once a plan is selected!

Who owns the website?

If you choose a hosting option at checkout, we will purchase and manage your domain and hosting for your convenience! The website code and content remain your property. At any time, you may request transfer of the domain and site to your own account, in which case Snowhoney Studios will provide all necessary files and instructions.

What if I want to host myself?

We will provide the finished website files and assist with initial deployment. Ongoing hosting, server maintenance, and troubleshooting will become your responsibility once deployed to your chosen provider. You will also need to find and purchase a domain name for your site.
