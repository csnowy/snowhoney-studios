Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Client information (business name, email, logo, domains, etc.).
  • Payment information via Stripe (we never store full card details).
  • Hosting-only customer details (business name, contact email, domain(s)).
  • Coupon and discount usage at checkout.
  • Temporary one-time codes used for client portal login (not stored in plaintext).

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Design and build websites.
  • Set up and manage hosting-only subscriptions.
  • Provide access to our client portal (for managing subscriptions and edits).
  • Apply valid coupons, discounts, or mockup credits.
  • Deliver extra page add-ons.
  • Process payments and comply with legal requirements.

3. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and analytics (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand site traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser if you prefer.

4. Sharing Information

We do not sell or rent data. We only share with trusted providers (Stripe, Netlify, Zoho Mail, SendGrid) to deliver our services.

5. Data Security

We rely on secure third-party providers and best practices. Clients must keep their portal codes, email accounts, and other credentials safe.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data, subject to legal or record-keeping obligations.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Updates will be posted here with a new “last updated” date.

8. Contact
