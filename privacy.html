Privacy Policy

Last updated: [Sept 17th, 2025]

1. Information We Collect

We collect client info (business name, email, logo, etc.), visitor info from site forms, and payment info via Stripe. We never store full card details.

2. How We Use Information

To build and maintain websites, manage hosting, provide business email, process payments, and improve our services.

2a. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and tracking tools (including Google Analytics) to understand site traffic and performance. This data may include IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer not to share this information.

2b. Marketing Emails

If you opt in to receive newsletters or marketing emails, we will use your email address for that purpose. You may unsubscribe at any time using the link included in every email.

3. Sharing Information

We do not sell or rent data. We only share with providers needed to deliver services (Stripe, Netlify, Zoho Mail, SendGrid).

4. Data Security

We rely on secure third-party providers and best practices. Clients must keep their own credentials safe.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data, subject to legal/record-keeping requirements.

6. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy and will post updates here.
